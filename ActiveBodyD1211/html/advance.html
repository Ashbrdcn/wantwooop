Major Workouts

Push-ups

A classic upper-body exercise that strengthens the chest, shoulders, triceps, and core.

Plyometric Push-ups

An explosive push-up variation that increases power and works the chest, shoulders, and triceps.

Chest Press

A compound exercise that targets the chest, shoulders, and triceps using a barbell or dumbbells.

Chest Fly

An isolation exercise that targets the chest muscles through controlled arm movements.

Bent-over Rows

A back and arm exercise, often using weights, that targets the lats, traps, and biceps.

Bodyweight Squats

A lower-body movement that targets the quads, hamstrings, glutes, and improves mobility.

Plank

A core-strengthening isometric hold that also engages the shoulders and stabilizing muscles.

Glute Bridges

A hip-thrusting exercise that strengthens the glutes, hamstrings, and lower back.

Bicycle Crunches

A core-focused movement that engages the obliques and rectus abdominis through twisting and leg motion.

Lunges

A versatile leg exercise that works the quads, hamstrings, glutes, and improves balance and coordination.

Russian Twists

A rotational core exercise that strengthens the obliques and improves torso stability.

Calf Raises

A lower-leg exercise that strengthens the calves and enhances ankle stability.
