Beginner Level Workouts and Meal Plans

Beginner Workout Plan

Follow this plan to build strength and endurance over six days, targeting all major muscle groups.

Monday
  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 10–12 reps
  • Bodyweight Squats: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets of 20 seconds hold
  • Glute Bridges: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Bent-over Rows (with water bottles): 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Arm Circles: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Wall Sit: 3 sets of 20 seconds
  • Side Plank: 2 sets of 15 seconds per side
Tuesday
  • Plyometric Push-ups: 3 sets of 8–10 reps
  • Walking Lunges: 3 sets of 12 reps per leg
  • Bicycle Crunches: 3 sets of 20 reps
  • Superman Hold: 3 sets of 15 seconds
  • Incline Push-ups: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Step-ups (using a sturdy surface): 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
  • Mountain Climbers: 3 sets of 20 reps
  • Calf Raises: 3 sets of 15 reps
Wednesday
  • Chest Press: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Reverse Lunges: 3 sets of 12 reps per leg
  • Plank Shoulder Taps: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Single-leg Deadlifts (using light weights): 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
  • Arm Raises (front and lateral): 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Russian Twists: 3 sets of 20 reps
  • Bird Dog: 3 sets of 10 reps per side
  • Wall Push-ups: 3 sets of 12 reps
Thursday
  • Incline Push-ups: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Step-ups: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
  • Bicycle Crunches: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Glute Bridges: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Side Plank: 2 sets of 15 seconds per side
  • Arm Circles: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Wall Sit: 3 sets of 20 seconds
  • Superman Hold: 3 sets of 15 seconds
Friday
  • Chest Fly: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Calf Raises: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Russian Twists: 3 sets of 20 reps
  • Plank Shoulder Taps: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Bent-over Rows: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Lunges: 3 sets of 12 reps per leg
  • Bird Dog: 3 sets of 10 reps per side
  • Mountain Climbers: 3 sets of 20 reps
Saturday
  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 10–12 reps
  • Bulgarian Split Squats: 3 sets of 8 reps per leg
  • Superman Hold: 3 sets of 15 seconds
  • Incline Push-ups: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Wall Push-ups: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Side Lunges: 3 sets of 10 reps per side
  • Reverse Crunches: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Bird Dog: 3 sets of 10 reps per side

Major Workouts

Push-ups

A classic upper-body exercise that strengthens the chest, shoulders, triceps, and core.

Bodyweight Squats

A lower-body movement that targets the quads, hamstrings, glutes, and improves mobility.

Plank

A core-strengthening isometric hold that also engages the shoulders and stabilizing muscles.

Glute Bridges

A hip-thrusting exercise that strengthens the glutes, hamstrings, and lower back.

Bent-over Rows

A back and arm exercise, often using weights or improvised tools, that targets the lats, traps, and biceps.

Lunges

A versatile leg exercise that works the quads, hamstrings, glutes, and improves balance and coordination.

Bicycle Crunches

A core-focused movement that engages the obliques and rectus abdominis through twisting and leg motion.

Mountain Climbers

A dynamic full-body exercise that increases heart rate while targeting the core, shoulders, and legs.

Russian Twists

A rotational core exercise that strengthens the obliques and improves torso stability.

Side Plank

A lateral core exercise that targets the obliques, hips, and improves overall core stability.

Calf Raises

A lower-leg exercise that strengthens the calves and enhances ankle stability.

Superman Hold

A prone position hold that targets the lower back, glutes, and hamstrings, improving posture and spinal stability.

Beginner Meal Plan

Day 1

Breakfast

Oatmeal with banana slices and a sprinkle of chia seeds

Lunch

Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and olive oil-lemon dressing.

Afternoon Snack

Greek yogurt topped with honey and granola.

Dinner

Baked salmon with steamed broccoli, carrots, and a side of quinoa

Day 2

Breakfast

Avocado toast on whole-grain bread with a boiled egg.

Lunch

Turkey wrap with lettuce, tomato, and hummus in a whole-wheat tortilla.

Afternoon Snack

Fresh fruit (apple or orange) with a handful of unsalted almonds.

Dinner

Stir-fried chicken breast with bell peppers, snap peas, and brown rice.

Day 3

Breakfast

Scrambled eggs with spinach and whole-grain toast.

Lunch

Tuna salad sandwich made with whole-grain bread and a side of sliced cucumbers.

Afternoon Snack

Carrot and celery sticks with hummus.

Dinner

Grilled lean beef patty with sweet potato and steamed green beans.
