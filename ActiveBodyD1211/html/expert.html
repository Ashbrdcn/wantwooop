Expert Workout Plan

Follow this advanced workout plan for six days, targeting all major muscle groups for strength, hypertrophy, and endurance.

Monday: Chest & Triceps
  • Barbell Bench Press: 4 sets of 6–8 reps
  • Incline Dumbbell Press: 4 sets of 8–10 reps
  • Chest Dips: 3 sets of 10–12 reps
  • Tricep Pushdowns: 4 sets of 12 reps
  • Close-Grip Bench Press: 4 sets of 8–10 reps
  • Diamond Push-ups: 3 sets of 12 reps
Tuesday: Back & Biceps
  • Deadlift: 4 sets of 5 reps
  • Pull-ups (Weighted if possible): 4 sets of 6–8 reps
  • Barbell Rows: 4 sets of 8–10 reps
  • Seated Cable Rows: 4 sets of 10–12 reps
  • Bicep Curls (EZ Bar): 4 sets of 10–12 reps
  • Hammer Curls: 3 sets of 12 reps
Wednesday: Legs & Abs
  • Back Squats: 4 sets of 6–8 reps
  • Leg Press: 4 sets of 10 reps
  • Walking Lunges: 3 sets of 12 reps per leg
  • Romanian Deadlifts: 4 sets of 8–10 reps
  • Leg Extensions: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Hanging Leg Raises: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Russian Twists (with weight): 3 sets of 20 reps
Thursday: Shoulders & Abs
  • Overhead Barbell Press: 4 sets of 6–8 reps
  • Dumbbell Lateral Raises: 4 sets of 12 reps
  • Arnold Press: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Front Raises (Dumbbell or Plate): 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Face Pulls: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Ab Wheel Rollouts: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Plank (Weighted if possible): 3 sets of 30–60 seconds
Friday: Full Body Strength
  • Clean and Press: 4 sets of 5 reps
  • Front Squats: 4 sets of 6–8 reps
  • Weighted Pull-ups: 3 sets of 6–8 reps
  • Barbell Deadlifts: 4 sets of 6 reps
  • Dumbbell Chest Press: 4 sets of 8–10 reps
  • Farmers Walk: 3 sets of 30 meters
Saturday: HIIT & Conditioning
  • Jump Rope: 5 minutes
  • Kettlebell Swings: 4 sets of 20 reps
  • Burpees: 4 sets of 15 reps
  • Mountain Climbers: 4 sets of 30 seconds
  • Sprints: 10 sets of 30 seconds work, 30 seconds rest
  • Battle Ropes: 3 sets of 20 seconds

Major Workouts

Barbell Bench Press

A compound exercise that targets the chest, shoulders, and triceps, building upper body strength.

Pull-ups

An upper body exercise that targets the back, shoulders, and arms, helping to improve pulling strength.

Deadlifts

A powerful compound movement that works the back, legs, and core, improving overall strength and stability.

Squats

A foundational lower body exercise that targets the quads, hamstrings, glutes, and improves overall leg strength.

Overhead Barbell Press

A shoulder exercise that targets the deltoids, triceps, and upper chest, improving upper body strength.

Barbell Rows

A back exercise that targets the lats, traps, and rhomboids, enhancing pulling strength and posture.

Incline Dumbbell Press

A chest exercise that targets the upper part of the chest and shoulders, helping to build a well-rounded chest.

Romanian Deadlifts

A hamstring-focused variation of the deadlift that targets the glutes, hamstrings, and lower back.

Chest Dips

A bodyweight exercise that primarily targets the chest and triceps, enhancing pushing strength.

Clean and Press

A full-body movement that targets multiple muscle groups, improving strength, power, and endurance.

Walking Lunges

A dynamic leg exercise that targets the quads, hamstrings, and glutes, enhancing balance and coordination.

Hanging Leg Raises

An ab-focused exercise that targets the lower abs and hip flexors, improving core strength and stability.

Expert Meal Plan

Day 1

Breakfast

Scrambled eggs with smoked salmon, avocado, and a side of sautéed spinach and mushrooms.

Lunch

Grilled chicken breast with quinoa, roasted sweet potatoes, and steamed broccoli, drizzled with olive oil and lemon.

Afternoon Snack

Greek yogurt with chia seeds, ground flaxseeds, mixed berries, and a handful of walnuts.

Dinner

Pan-seared salmon with a side of asparagus, quinoa, and roasted Brussels sprouts with balsamic vinegar.

Day 2

Breakfast

Oats topped with almond butter, chia seeds, banana slices, and a sprinkle of cinnamon.

Lunch

Tuna salad made with mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, avocado, and a balsamic vinaigrette dressing.

Afternoon Snack

Protein smoothie made with whey protein, almond milk, spinach, frozen berries, and a tablespoon of peanut butter.

Dinner

Grilled steak with roasted root vegetables (carrots, parsnips, beets), and sautéed kale.

Day 3

Breakfast

Egg white omelette with spinach, mushrooms, and feta cheese, served with a side of whole-grain toast.

Lunch

Grilled chicken quinoa bowl with roasted vegetables (zucchini, bell peppers), and tahini sauce.

Afternoon Snack

A handful of almonds, dark chocolate, and a green apple.

Dinner

Grilled chicken thighs with wild rice and a side of sautéed spinach and garlic.
